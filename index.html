A Bit More About Me

My expertise lies in R, and I am also proficient in Python. With a Master's in Global Health from the University of Geneva and a Bachelor's in Biology from Vassar College, I bring a rigorous analytical and scientific approach to my work.

My professional journey includes roles as a Researcher at the University of Geneva, a Contact Tracing Analyst for the WHO, and an educator. I am passionate about leveraging technology to solve complex problems and communicating data-driven insights effectively.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity

This 8-week live cohort course focuses on leveraging no-code AI tools to automate tasks and enhance productivity in professional and research environments.

Key Features:

  • Weekly live workshops & help sessions
  • Private community forums
  • Personalized feedback on assessments
  • Capstone project coaching
  • Shareable certificate upon completion
  • Lifetime access to a rich tutorial library

Course Modules Include:

  • Websites, Documents & Presentations
  • AI-Automated Data Analysis
  • GenAI for Research & Text Analysis
  • Custom GPTs and Chatbots
  • AI for Task Automation
  • GenAI for Communication and Outreach
